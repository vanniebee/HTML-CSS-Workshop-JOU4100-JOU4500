General Instructions

The game is very simple. You need to win every game you participate in to stay alive. If you or the team you are in lose a game you die immediately. The frontman announces the beginning of every game and gives instructions on how the game will be played. The games are timed and part of winning is completing the game within the allocated time. There will be six games in total.

Games Played

  1. Red Light, Green Light
  2. Honeycomb Candy
  3. Tug of War
  4. Glass Bridge
  5. Marbles
  6. Squid Game
