Concept of the Game

Masked men in red jumpsuits, a bunch of broke hostages, and a set of games you may have played in elementary school — this is the premise of one of the most popular Netflix shows today. Don't forget to add in a lot of blood, gore, and billions in cash and you get Squid Game, the Netflix show taking the world by storm. The south korean drama follows people competing for money in violent games and has captured the imagination of viewers of all age groups.

In or out?

The contestants of the game all have one thing in common. They all owe a large amount of debt and need this money to save their lives. They are carefully selected and given the option to either join the game or stay in debt. The rules of the game seem fair, but what they do not know is if you lose a game you die.
